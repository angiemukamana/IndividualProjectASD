Our History


How it all began

Nestled in the heart of a bustling city, Angie's African Bistro is more than just a restaurant; it's a vibrant tapestry of history and culture. Founded in 2020 by Angie Mukamana, The African Table began as a small eatery offering traditional East African dishes to the local community. Angie's vision was to create a space where the rich culinary heritage of Africa could be celebrated and shared with the world. Over the years, The African Table has grown into a renowned establishment, known for its authentic flavors, lively atmosphere, and cultural events that highlight African music, dance, and art. The restaurant's walls are adorned with traditional African art and artifacts, each piece telling a story of its origins and the diverse cultures that make up the African continent. Today, The African Table stands as a testament to the enduring spirit and rich traditions of Africa, offering diners not just a meal, but a journey through the continent's vibrant history.
